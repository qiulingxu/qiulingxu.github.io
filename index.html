Hi, all! I am a fifth-year Ph.D. student at Purdue Computer Science. I am fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Xiangyu Zhang and co-advised by Prof. Jean Honorio. Before coming to Purdue, I received my B.S. from National Elite Program at Nanjing University and was supervised by Prof. Yang Yu. I also collaborated with Xin Lu from Adobe and Pannaga Shivaswamy from Netflix.
My PhD research focuses on Robust Machine Learning. Recently, I also did work in Recommendation System, Multi-Modality, Reinforcement Learning, Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, Logical Reasoning and Out-of-distribution Generalization. I am looking for jobs requiring impactful and innovative product or academical research.
