Research : Variance Reduced Policy Gradient

Reinforcement Learning Research
Nov 2016 - Mar 2017
This work aims to provide fail-safe or robust guarantee for basic reinforcement learning algorithm. I try to limit variance of Q & V value each step to provide a lower policy bound , using Chebyshev Inequality. Soft penalty to large variance is merged into goal. Similar to policy gradient method, monte-carlo estimation formula with baseline trick is provided to estimate the penalty.
